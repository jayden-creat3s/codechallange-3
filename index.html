Marvel's Enduring Legacy: From Timely to Today Let's rewind to the genesis of the Marvel Age. August 1961 saw the birth of The Fantastic Four and a slew of other iconic superhero titles, spearheaded by the legendary Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, and Steve Ditko Marvel Comics. These visionaries crafted a universe teeming with heroes and villains, many of whom call New York City home. The Marvel brand, subtly present in previous years, was cemented as the company's primary identifier. This era introduced characters who are now household names, including Spider-Man, Wolverine, Iron Man, Captain America, Hulk, Thor, Doctor Strange, Daredevil, Black Panther, and Captain Marvel Marvel Comics.
